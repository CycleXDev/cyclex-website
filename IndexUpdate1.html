Vision & Architecture

Security-First Architecture

CycleX is built around a simple rule: critical actions should be enforced by code ‚Äî not by promises.

The ecosystem pairs on-chain determinism (fixed rules, predictable execution) with a growing security toolkit that helps users inspect risk signals, approvals, liquidity, and transaction behavior.

Treasury logic is constrained by the contract. There is no ‚Äúmanual payout button‚Äù and no discretionary control over user claims. Distribution, burns, and defined operational allocations are the only permitted flows.

REWARDS MODEL

Rewards Model

CycleX operates on a quarterly reward cycle. Each cycle is executed automatically according to predefined on-chain rules.

Distribution Logic

  • 9% rewards are distributed to eligible holders.
  • For every reward cycle, the treasury burns an amount equal to the 9% distributed rewards (1:1).
  • An additional 20% of the 9% reward amount is allocated to Development & Operations ‚Äî sourced from the treasury only.
Example:
For every 1 CYCX distributed as rewards, 0.2 CYCX is allocated to Development & Operations.
Holders always receive their full 9% reward.
Fully automated ‚Ä¢ On-chain enforced ‚Ä¢ Publicly verifiable

Eligibility & Claim Rules

  • Tokens must be held for at least 60 days in a self-custody Web3 wallet.
  • Exchange balances do not qualify.
  • Rewards must be claimed every cycle.
    Unclaimed rewards do not roll over.

On-Chain Enforcement

Rewards are claimed directly by users via a secure Merkle-proof system. There is no admin, no middleman, and no manual intervention.

Each holder triggers their own on-chain claim using their own wallet and pays their own gas.

Policy:
Reward cycles are executed quarterly ‚Äî end of March, June, September, and December, at 20:00 UK time.
Future-Ready Protocol

Future-Ready Protocol

CycleX is engineered to prioritize holder protection, with a fixed supply cap and a treasury governed exclusively by deterministic on-chain logic ‚Äî not by individuals, administrators, or discretionary authority.

CycleX has a fixed supply and a long-term participation model. As awareness grows, available circulating supply can become harder to find because many holders may prefer to keep their position to remain eligible for future cycles. This is not a promise of price appreciation ‚Äî market conditions and outcomes are uncertain.

If the main treasury is eventually exhausted after many years of reward cycles, the protocol may evolve only through a community-approved upgrade or a successor contract that preserves the same core principles: transparent execution, no surprise minting, and no hidden control over user funds.

Any future evolution will be structured to avoid unfair dilution and to maintain the long-term integrity, scarcity, and transparency upon which CycleX is built.
