Vision & Architecture

Vision & On-Chain Security Architecture

CycleX is built on a simple but powerful principle: let code, math, and cryptography enforce the rules â€” not human promises.

Our ecosystem is engineered to be transparent, reward-based, and resistant to exploitation. Rewards are earned by holders who stay committed long term, while the protocol continuously burns tokens to reinforce scarcity and long-term value.

Treasury funds are permanently locked inside the smart contract. The developer cannot withdraw, redirect, or override the treasury. Only reward distributions, burns, and Development & Operations allocations are permitted by code.

REWARDS MODEL

Rewards Model

CycleX operates on a quarterly reward cycle. Each cycle is executed automatically according to predefined on-chain rules.

Distribution Logic

  • 9% rewards are distributed to eligible holders.
  • For every reward cycle, the treasury burns an amount equal to the 9% distributed rewards (1:1).
  • An additional 20% of the 9% reward amount is allocated to Development & Operations â€” sourced from the treasury only.
Example:
For every 1 CYCX distributed as rewards, 0.2 CYCX is allocated to Development & Operations.
Holders always receive their full 9% reward.

Eligibility & Holding Rules

To be included in a reward cycle, you must hold CYCLEX correctly and respect the 60-day Web3 holding rule:

  1. Hold in a self-custody Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Ledger) for at least 60 days.
  2. Exchange balances do not count until tokens are withdrawn to your own Web3 wallet.
  3. Snapshots are taken before each cycle; moving or selling before snapshot removes eligibility.
  4. Rewards are based on duration + balance only, not on buy price or trading history.
  5. Claims use Merkle-proof and can only be triggered by the owner wallet.

By continuing, you confirm that you understand how eligibility is calculated and acknowledge these rules before interacting with the reward system.
