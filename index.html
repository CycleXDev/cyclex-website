The Problem

Your Wallet Is More Exposed Than You Think

Most crypto wallets are silently vulnerable. Forgotten approvals, unlimited spenders, and malicious contracts sit undetected — until it’s too late. You don’t get hacked. You get drained through doors you left open.

  • Unlimited Approvals: one forgotten contract can drain everything you own.
  • No Real-Time Alerts: most wallets provide little to no warning on risky interactions.
  • Invisible Exposure: your blast radius — how much can be taken — is unknown.

CycleX Firewall was built to close these gaps. CYCX token holders unlock advanced threat controls and may earn quarterly rewards (if eligible) for staying in the ecosystem.

Security Infrastructure

Built Like a Security Company, Not a Token

CycleX is built on one principle: let code, cryptography, and math enforce the rules — not human promises. The firewall is the product. The token is the access key.

Eligibility & Holding Rules

To be included in a reward cycle, you must hold CYCLEX correctly and respect the 60-day Web3 holding rule:

  1. Hold in a self-custody Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Ledger) for at least 60 days.
  2. Exchange balances do not count until tokens are withdrawn to your own Web3 wallet.
  3. Snapshots are taken before each cycle; moving or selling before snapshot removes eligibility.
  4. Rewards are based on duration + balance only, not on buy price or trading history.
  5. Claims use Merkle-proof and can only be triggered by the owner wallet.

By continuing, you confirm that you understand how eligibility is calculated and acknowledge these rules before interacting with the reward system.
